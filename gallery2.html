Cannonball

Cannonball is a robot I created for my rhythm game projects. He was inspired by Robo Mario and many other robots. From the years I've drawn Mario and robots, in 2020, I wanted to make a Mario-like robot myself. I went through iterations for the design before settling on this one.

Image Year Comments
2020 Initial design. Has Mario's nose and mustache.
2021 New design. Changed head to be more original but still have the Mario-like feel to it.
